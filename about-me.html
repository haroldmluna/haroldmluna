Hola! I'm Harold Luna, a New Yorker with a zest for digital magic and a flair for storytelling. Born in the vibrant rhythms of Santo Domingo and molded in the hustle of New York, I've combined my Dominican roots with Manhattan's pulse to craft digital masterpieces.

From co-founding studios in the Caribbean to skyrocketing sales in the Big Apple, my journey is as diverse as the city I call home. My passion? Breathing life into digital content. With a reel of award-winning films and an entrepreneurial spirit that has driven businesses to flourish, I've honed the art of making pixels dance to the music of success.

When I'm not orchestrating video commercials or diving deep into marketing analytics, you can find me connecting with fellow leaders and innovators. From the classrooms of NYU to the challenging terrains of sales and project management, I'm always on the lookout for the next big adventure. So, if you're ready to create, innovate, or just chat over a cup of café con leche, drop me a line. Let's make digital magic together!
